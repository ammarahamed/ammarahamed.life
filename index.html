About Me

Here's a little about the person behind the work.

I'm a growth marketer with product and brand marketing experience across agency and in-house settings. From executive communications to customer engagement — I've worked with some of the best brands and startups in South Asia, the UK, and globally.

From my first career as a digital marketer to the interview-inspired journalist to the copywriting and content creation work I do today — there's nothing I love more than sitting down with another human to uncover the story and heart behind their work.

Currently, I'm managing marketing and growth at Vector Agents — an AI company building digital employees with human outcomes. I'm also building Cocoon, a platform for upskilling and reskilling talent for the future. And I continue to co-lead Buildin Blocks, the marketing agency I co-founded during the 2020 lockdowns.

I love to speak, write, and connect. I play basketball, hockey, and swim. I've always loved a hike to the mountains, a visit to the beach, or a ride to the countryside on a motorbike.
